# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

UrbanFresh serves wholesalers, distributors, food-service buyers, merchant exporters, institutional buyers, and rice brands that need bulk basmati rice from a mill-side supplier. Visitors may be comparing several suppliers quickly, often on a phone, and need to judge product fit, location, responsiveness, and commercial credibility before starting a conversation.

## Product Purpose

The website turns serious rice-buying intent into complete, useful quotation enquiries for UrbanFresh Rice Mills in Karnal. Success means a buyer understands the available numbered basmati varieties, trusts the business enough to make contact, and submits the product, quantity, packaging, destination, and timeline needed for a productive first response.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, trustworthy, practical, and trade-focused. The voice should sound like an experienced mill representative who knows which details matter, answers plainly, and does not inflate claims.

## Anti-references

- Flashy startup landing pages with decorative gradients, glass effects, or exaggerated animation.
- Luxury-food clichés that make a working rice mill feel like a fashion brand.
- BI Agri Foods imitation or copied competitor branding.
- Obvious AI layouts built from repeated identical cards, tiny labels, and generic marketing phrases.
- Unsupported claims about quality, capacity, certifications, export history, or guaranteed availability.

## Design Principles

1. Show the product early. Realistic rice imagery and clear variety names should do more work than decorative graphics.
2. Sound like a person in the trade. Use direct sentences, concrete buyer details, and natural calls to action.
3. Earn trust through specificity. Explain what buyers should send and what UrbanFresh will confirm without inventing proof.
4. Make quotation easy on mobile. Keep the path to WhatsApp and the quote form clear, short, and resilient.
5. Use restraint with intent. Strong typography, useful imagery, and varied rhythm should replace repetitive card grids and template decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain readable contrast, visible keyboard focus, labelled form controls, clear validation messages, touch-friendly controls, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that remain usable with text zoom.
