Basmati rice from India

Bulk rice enquiries for overseas buyers.

UrbanFresh in Karnal welcomes requirements from importers, distributors, food-service suppliers and rice brands outside India.

Start with the destination market

An overseas rice enquiry becomes useful when it identifies the destination country, expected volume, preferred variety, processing style, packaging format and buying timeline. If you have a destination port or trade term preference, include that too.

Export feasibility, documentation, testing, labelling, logistics and commercial terms depend on the destination and order. They must be reviewed and confirmed for each enquiry.

Rice options to discuss

Buyers can ask about 1121 Basmati, 1718 Basmati, 1509 Basmati and Traditional Basmati. Processing requests may include Raw/White, Steam, Sella/Parboiled or Golden Sella, subject to confirmation.

For importers and distributors

Share the target customer, current sourcing format, preferred pack size, approximate volume and reorder expectations. This helps separate an exploratory enquiry from an immediate procurement requirement.

For overseas private labels

Add brand position, intended retail pack size, packaging material, artwork status, labelling needs and destination market. Visit the private-label page for a fuller brief.
