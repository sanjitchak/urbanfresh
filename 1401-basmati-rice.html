Request 1401 Basmati for your market

UrbanFresh accepts requirement-led enquiries for 1401 Basmati rice from wholesalers, food-service buyers, merchant exporters and packaged-rice brands. A useful request includes the required processing, quantity, packaging, destination and purchase timeline.

Always confirm current availability, crop, processing style, buyer-approved specification, packing, documentation and commercial terms. Website information does not replace an accepted order specification.

Build a useful 1401 enquiry

Choose the processing style

State whether you require Steam, Sella/Parboiled, Golden Sella or Raw/White rice. If you are unsure, describe the target market, cooking use and preferred presentation.

Add order volume and frequency

Share the approximate quantity for the first order and whether the requirement is one-off, seasonal or expected to repeat.

Define packaging and destination

Include the bag size and delivery city, country or destination port. Private-label buyers should also share packaging material, artwork status and target market.
