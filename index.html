Quality and registrations

Checks begin before paddy enters our plant.

Our quality process covers field procurement, drying, storage, pre-cleaning, de-stoning, grading, paddy separation, metal detection and trained supervision.

Our available documents include ISO 22000:2018, FSSAI, APEDA, U.S. FDA registration, Importer Exporter Code and rice-mill registrations. Buyers can request current copies and verify their scope for the intended order.
