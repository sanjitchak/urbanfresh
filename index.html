Buyer questions

What to know before you request a quote.

What information should I send for a rice quote?

Share the variety, processing style, approximate quantity, pack size, delivery city or country, buyer type and purchase timeline. If you are unsure about a field, say so and ask us to advise.

Can I ask for Steam, Sella or Golden Sella rice?

Yes. Add the processing style to your enquiry. Current availability and exact commercial specifications should be confirmed for every order.

Does UrbanFresh handle private-label enquiries?

Yes, private-label and buyer-specified packaging enquiries are welcome. Share target pack size, packaging material, design readiness, quantity and destination.

Do you supply outside Haryana?

UrbanFresh welcomes enquiries from buyers across India and from overseas markets. Feasibility and terms depend on the order requirement and destination.
