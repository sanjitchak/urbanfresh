Frequently asked

Private-label questions.

Can UrbanFresh quote without finished packaging artwork?

Yes. State that the artwork is not yet final. Commercial feasibility still depends on the product, pack, quantity and destination.

Can I request more than one pack size?

Yes. List every intended size and estimated quantity separately so the enquiry can be evaluated clearly.

Is there a fixed minimum order quantity?

This website does not publish an unverified universal MOQ. Send your required quantity and packing format for a requirement-specific response.
